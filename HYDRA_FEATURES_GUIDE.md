## Feature 1: Campaign Management

### Your Content Command Center

Every piece of content you create lives inside a **Campaign**. Think of campaigns as containers that hold everything related to a specific marketing initiative.

### What You Can Do:

**Create Campaigns For:**
- New single or album release promotions
- Fan account content series
- Merchandise tie-in campaigns
- Artist milestone celebrations
- Seasonal or holiday promotions

**Configure Your Campaign:**
- Select the artist (with their brand guidelines automatically applied)
- Set target countries for localized content
- Define campaign dates (start → end)
- Attach budget codes for tracking spend
- Track status: Draft → Active → Completed → Archived

**Upload Campaign Assets:**
- Reference images (visual inspiration)
- Audio tracks (songs, backing music)
- Product images (albums, photocards, merchandise)
- Existing videos for reference

### Campaign Dashboard Shows:
- Total assets uploaded
- Videos generated (completed, processing, failed)
- Posts scheduled and published
- Total views and engagement across all content

## Feature 3: Video Creation

### Four Ways to Create

Hydra offers multiple creation modes to match your workflow and needs:

---

### Mode A: Quick Create (Speed First)

**Best for:** Testing ideas rapidly, high-volume creation

**Time:** 1-2 minutes

**How it works:**
1. Enter a simple prompt ("girl dancing in neon city")
2. Click generate
3. Get your video

Minimal configuration, maximum speed.

---

### Mode B: Generate (Full AI Video)

**Best for:** High-quality, customized AI video synthesis

**Control everything:**
- **Text prompt** – Describe what you want
- **Negative prompt** – Specify what to avoid
- **Duration** – How long (typically up to 5 seconds)
- **Aspect ratio** – 9:16 (mobile), 1:1 (square), 16:9 (widescreen)
- **Reference image** – Guide the visual style
- **Style preset** – Apply cinematic, lo-fi, neon, vintage looks
- **Merchandise** – Feature specific products in the video
- **Trending keywords** – Apply up to 3 trends

**Smart Features:**
- AI optimizes your prompt for best results
- Celebrity detection warnings (avoid rights issues)
- Quality scoring after generation

---

### Mode C: Compose (Slideshow Creator)

**Best for:** Using your own assets, brand-controlled creation

**Perfect when you have:**
- Existing images you want to animate
- A specific song to feature
- Promotional materials to repurpose

**Workflow:**
1. Select an audio track from your campaign
2. Define scenes/text with timing for each
3. Upload or auto-search images for each scene
4. Add transitions and effects
5. Generate polished video with music

---

### Mode D: Batch Generation (Scale)

**Best for:** Creating multiple variations to test performance

**What you can vary:**
- Base concept (same idea, different executions)
- Style presets (cinematic vs lo-fi vs vibrant)
- Merchandise (Album A vs Album B)
- Audio tracks (different songs)

**Output:** Multiple video versions to compare and select winners.

## Feature 6: Multi-Platform Publishing

### One Dashboard, All Platforms

Connect and publish to your social accounts:

| Platform | Features |
|----------|----------|
| **TikTok** | Full API integration, duet/stitch settings, music options |
| **Instagram** | Reels publishing, feed posts |
| **YouTube** | Shorts publishing |
| **Twitter** | Video tweets |

### Publishing Workflow

1. **Connect Accounts**
   - OAuth login for each platform
   - Accounts tied to specific labels (access control)
   - Secure token management

2. **Create a Scheduled Post**
   - Select your generated video
   - Write platform-specific captions
   - Add hashtags (AI suggests trending ones)
   - Choose which account to publish to
   - Set date and time

3. **Platform-Specific Settings**
   - TikTok: Allow duets? Allow stitches?
   - Geographic targeting
   - Age restrictions if needed

4. **Schedule or Publish Now**
   - Queue for optimal posting times
   - Batch schedule (Mon/Wed/Fri for 2 weeks)
   - Immediate publish option

### Auto-Published Content Tracking

After publishing, Hydra automatically syncs:
- View counts
- Likes
- Comments
- Shares
- Saves
- Engagement rate

Updated regularly so you see real performance.
